THE 'BLACK-POTTERY' EXPERIENCE AT LARNAI





About the Craft

Various sizes of pottery exist, from small to big pots with a variety of various containers, vessels, and jars. The pottery after hardening gets shades of black and brown. Due to the pots breaking while burning process, natural flower dyes are used. The texture of pottery is generally rough since most of the work is done physically and is smoothened using various tools.
